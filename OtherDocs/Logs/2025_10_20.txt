I am downloading yt-dlp now:
brew install yt-dlp ffmpeg

How to use:
    for audio download:
        yt-dlp -f bestaudio -o "dail_session.mp3" https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=abcdef12345

    for video download:
        yt-dlp -f bestvideo+bestaudio -o "dail_session.mp4" https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=abcdef12345

Use ffmpeg to get snippets without re-encoding:
    ffmpeg -ss 00:10:45 -to 00:11:15 -i dail_session.mp3 -acodec copy "dublin_td_clip1.mp3"

Convert to same type:
    ffmpeg -i input.mp3 -ac 1 -ar 16000 output.wav
